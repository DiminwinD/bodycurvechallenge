# BodyCurve Product

BodyCurve is a fitness ecosystem with two main products:

1. BodyCurve Challenge
A guided transformation program with workouts, motivation, and structured support.

2. BodyCurve Control
A nutrition and calorie tracking app designed to help users monitor food intake and progress.

Core business goal:
Capture leads, convert them into challenge participants, and later expand into a SaaS fitness ecosystem.

Immediate MVP goal:
Landing page email capture → store in Supabase → send confirmation/PDF.
